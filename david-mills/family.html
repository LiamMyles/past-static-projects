In Memory of Theresa "Tracy" Mills

1950-1980

A devoted wife and a loving friend cut off her in prime. We know you could have done so much. You will be missed.

Family

David's family was small he was an only child and had no aunts or uncles. He was very close to Tracy's family; however after her death they blamed him for bringing her to the city and cut off contact with him.

David always regarded his dogs are his kids and thought of them as family. Over time David even considered William as part of the family even looking after him when his age started to affect his health. After Williams death David met a girl who he would latter marry and have two kids with.
